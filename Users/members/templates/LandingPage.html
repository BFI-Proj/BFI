BALANCE FOOD INTAKE

This web-based application project is for Filipinos to have knowledge about what benefits or harm does a specific dish can give.
It aims to help Filipinos improve their health and be physically fit. The web-based application contains different kinds of food with a bunch of dishes.
When users click on a particular dish, they will access detailed information about the food, including the potential health benefits or risks associated with consuming it.
